Binding Options

Plastic Comb Bind

Comb binding is a method that enables a book to lay flat when open. The plastic "combs" come in  varying sizes up to a maximum thickness of approximately 350 sheets (700 pages) of 20# paper.

Requires card stock covers on front & back.

Additional Service Charge For Comb Binding.

Shrink Wrap

Shrink wrap is a way to package multiple pages for distribution. Perfect for drilled 3 hole paper later to be placed in a binder. Shrink wrapping is limited to a maximum thickness of 500 sheets (1000 pages) of 20# paper.

Black Tape Bind

Black tape or thermal tape binding is limited to on-demand printing jobs. This method of finishing provides an economical and strong binding limited to 8-1/2" x 11" documents. Maximum thickness is limited to 120 sheets (240 pages) of 20# paper.

Requires card stock covers on front & back.
